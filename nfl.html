Welcome to the NFL

The National Football League (NFL) is the pinnacle of American football, featuring 32 teams across two conferences — the AFC and NFC. Each team brings its own legacy, fanbase, and fierce competition to the field. Whether you're following the dynasties or rooting for underdogs, the NFL delivers unforgettable moments every season.

Use the sidebar to explore each conference and dive into team breakdowns. You’ll find division listings, team names, and links to college football for a full view of the sport’s landscape.
